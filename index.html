I work with professionals who need to use English with precision and authority — in negotiations, presentations, legal and regulatory contexts, and high-level examinations.

My formation is literary and philological: Aberdeen, Oxford, and doctoral research at the University of Zürich. I am interested in how language carries meaning, not just how it functions. That interest shapes the way I work.

Twenty-five years of teaching and examining across Europe, working with corporate professionals, legal practitioners, military personnel, and university candidates. Accredited Cambridge Assessment Oral Examiner for C1 Advanced and C2 Proficiency.

British. Based in Switzerland. Bilingual in English and Italian.
